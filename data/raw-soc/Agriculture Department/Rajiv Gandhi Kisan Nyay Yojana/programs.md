## Rajiv Gandhi Kisan Nyay Yojana
- BUDGET: Chhattisgarh State Budget 2025-26 allocates ₹5,600 crores for Rajiv Gandhi Kisan Nyay Yojana direct benefit transfer. Budget allocation covers rice (₹500/quintal), maize (₹400/quintal), soybean (₹1,000/quintal), groundnut (₹800/quintal). Processing: Automated through state agriculture portal. Annual fund transfer to farmer accounts through e-governance system.
- SURVEY: Chhattisgarh Economic Survey 2024-25: 'Rajiv Gandhi Kisan Nyay Yojana provides direct cash benefit based on crop production. Scheme ensures farmer income support through input cost recovery. Beneficiary farmers: 21.4 lakh (paddy), 8.2 lakh (other crops). State contribution: ₹5,200 crores annually. Scheme operational since 2020, expanded to 5 crops in 2023.'
- OUTCOME: Outcome Budget 2025-26 performance metrics: Total beneficiaries: 29.6 lakh farmers. Direct benefit transfer (DBT) processed: ₹4,850 crores (FY 2024-25). Payment success rate: 98.2%. Average benefit per farmer: ₹16,400 (annual). Crop coverage: Paddy (main), maize, soybean, groundnut, cotton. Impact: Farm income increased average 18-22%. Farmer retention: 95% continue cultivation.
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official Notification No. 26-A-2020/CGN dated 21.05.2020 establishes Rajiv Gandhi Kisan Nyay Yojana. Notification details: Eligible crops (paddy, maize, soybean, groundnut, cotton). Benefit amount per crop per quintal. Beneficiary verification: Land ownership + production proof. Payment process: Direct transfer to farmer bank accounts. Notification amended 2023 to include 5 crops (from original 2).
- CAG: CAG State Accounts Report 2024-25 audits direct benefit transfer implementation. Audit findings: Total DBT amount: ₹4,850 crores (FY 2024-25). Payment accuracy: 98.8%. Duplicate payment prevention: Zero cases. Bank reconciliation: Completed. Fund availability: Maintained throughout fiscal year. Compliance with RBI/NEFT guidelines: 100%. Report classifies RGKNY implementation as 'Excellent' with zero audit objections.
- PORTAL: Rajiv Gandhi Kisan Nyay Yojana featured on National Portal of India under Agriculture > Income Support > Direct Benefits. Portal description: 'Chhattisgarh state scheme providing direct cash support to farmers based on crop production.' Portal features: Beneficiary portal (rgkny.cg.nic.in) for status tracking, payment history, grievance redressal. Statistics shown: 29.6 lakh beneficiaries, ₹4,850 cr annual disbursement. Cross-referenced with PM Fasal Bima Yojana integration.
