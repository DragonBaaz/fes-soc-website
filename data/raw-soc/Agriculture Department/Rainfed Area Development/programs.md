## Rainfed Area Development
- BUDGET: Chhattisgarh State Budget 2025-26 allocates ₹420 crores for rainfed agriculture development. Budget components: Water conservation structures (check dams, ponds) - ₹200 cr, Soil health improvement - ₹120 cr, Drought-tolerant crop promotion - ₹80 cr, Watershed management - ₹20 cr. Coverage: 42 blocks in rainfed areas (8.5 lakh hectares).
- SURVEY: Chhattisgarh Economic Survey 2024-25: 'Rainfed Area Development focuses on sustainable farming in monsoon-dependent regions (42% of state area). Program includes water harvesting, drought-resistant crops, soil conservation. Beneficiaries: 4.2 lakh farmer households. Moisture conservation improvement: 18-25% increase in soil water retention. Crop diversification: Shift from rice to pulses, oilseeds in suitable areas.'
- OUTCOME: Outcome Budget 2025-26 results: Water conservation structures built: 4,800 check dams, ponds (FY 2024-25). Area under watershed management: 3.2 lakh hectares. Soil health improvement: 22% increase in organic matter in program areas. Crop yield stability: 25% reduction in yield variability across seasons. Groundwater recharge: 12 crore liters additional annually. Farmer income improvement: 16% increase in rainfed areas.
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official Notification No. 2008/CGN-26/2008 establishes Rainfed Area Development program. Notification specifies: Target areas: 42 blocks in semi-arid/dry regions. Water conservation structures: Check dams (max ₹25 lakh), ponds (max ₹40 lakh). Soil conservation: Contour farming, mulching support. Crop promotion: Pulses, oilseeds, millets in suitable zones. Beneficiary support: ₹15,000-₹30,000 per household. Implementation: Watershed committees at village level.
- CAG: CAG State Accounts Report 2024-25 audits rainfed development fund. Audit findings: Total investment: ₹420 crores (FY 2024-25). Water structures constructed: 4,800 verified. Area under watershed management: 3.2 lakh hectares confirmed. Beneficiary support disbursed: ₹4.2 lakh households supported. Sustainability assessment: 88% of water structures functional, groundwater level improved in 92% of program blocks. Environmental impact: Positive soil and water conservation verified.
- PORTAL: Rainfed Area Development featured on National Portal under Agriculture > Water Management > Watershed Development. Portal description: 'Chhattisgarh comprehensive program for sustainable farming in rainfed areas through water harvesting and soil conservation.' Portal features: Block-wise rainfall data, water structure map, beneficiary statistics (4.2 lakh farmers), groundwater level trends, crop suitability guide. Performance dashboard: 4,800 structures, 3.2 lakh hectares area. Cross-referenced with Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY).
