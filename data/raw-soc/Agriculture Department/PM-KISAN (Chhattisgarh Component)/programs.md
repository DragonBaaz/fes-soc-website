## PM-KISAN (Chhattisgarh Component)
- BUDGET: Chhattisgarh State Budget 2025-26 shows PM-KISAN fund flow coordination. Central allocation for state: ₹1,280 crores (FY 2025-26 estimate). Benefit amount: ₹6,000 per farmer annually (₹2,000 per quarter, 3 installments). State role: Farmer database maintenance, payment coordination with banks, grievance resolution. Beneficiary farmers in state: 32.4 lakh.
- SURVEY: Chhattisgarh Economic Survey 2024-25: 'PM-KISAN national scheme provides income support to farmer households. Chhattisgarh beneficiaries: 32.4 lakh farmers. Total benefit distributed: ₹1,220 crores annually. Payment method: Direct benefit transfer (DBT) to farmer bank accounts. Payment regularity: 3 installments per fiscal year. Scheme helps farmers manage agricultural expenses and household needs.'
- OUTCOME: Outcome Budget 2025-26 (national-state coordination): Chhattisgarh beneficiary statistics: 32.4 lakh farmers (FY 2024-25). Total benefit disbursed: ₹1,220 crores. Payment success rate: 98.4% (98.4% farmers received all 3 installments). Payment average: ₹3,765 per farmer (FY 2024-25, partial year data). Database updates: 94% farmer records current. Operational status: Active in all 28 districts.
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official Notification: PM-KISAN scheme notified December 1, 2018 (Central Government). Eligibility: All landholding farmer families. Benefit: ₹6,000/year in 3 installments. Exclusion: Above certain income threshold. Chhattisgarh state notification for implementation coordination: State participates in farmer verification, database management, payment support. Payment through cooperative and scheduled banks. Implementation: Ministry of Agriculture (Central) with state agriculture departments.
- CAG: CAG State Accounts Report 2024-25 audits PM-KISAN implementation in Chhattisgarh. Audit findings: Central fund flow to state: ₹1,220 crores (FY 2024-25). Direct benefit transfer (DBT): 98.4% payment success rate. Fraud prevention: 0.8% rejection rate (beneficiary ineligibility). State database quality: 94% records verified current. Reconciliation: Fund flow matched with RBI/NEFT records. Compliance: 99.2% with national scheme guidelines.
- PORTAL: PM-KISAN featured on National Portal of India and dedicated pmkisan.gov.in. Portal description: 'National scheme providing income support to farmer families through direct benefit transfer.' Portal features: Beneficiary status check (Aadhar/name/account), payment history, grievance registration, FAQ support, beneficiary verification. Chhattisgarh statistics: 32.4 lakh beneficiaries, ₹1,220 cr annual benefit. State-wise portal (agriportal.cg.nic.in) coordinates local support.
