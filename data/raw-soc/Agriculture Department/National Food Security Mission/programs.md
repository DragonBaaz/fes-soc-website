## National Food Security Mission
- BUDGET: Chhattisgarh State Budget 2025-26 allocates ₹95 crores for National Food Security Mission (NFSM) state component. Budget allocation: Pulses production support - ₹45 cr, Oilseeds enhancement - ₹35 cr, Training and extension - ₹10 cr, Seed distribution - ₹5 cr. Coverage: Pulses (26 districts), Oilseeds (12 districts).
- SURVEY: Chhattisgarh Economic Survey 2024-25: 'National Food Security Mission focuses on food grain production enhancement. State component targets pulse and oilseed production. Beneficiaries: 1.2 lakh farmers in pulses, 85,000 in oilseeds. Pulse production (2024-25): 18.5 lakh MT. Oilseed production: 4.2 lakh MT. Contribution to national food security: Chhattisgarh supplies 12% national pulse output.'
- OUTCOME: Outcome Budget 2025-26 results (state component): Pulse production (FY 2024-25): 18.5 lakh MT (6% increase YoY). Oilseed production: 4.2 lakh MT (8% increase YoY). High-yielding variety seed distribution: 4,200 MT. Improved seed adoption: 78% among beneficiary farmers. Productivity improvement: 18% increase in supported crops. Farmer income from pulses/oilseeds: ₹28,400 average annually per household.
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official Notification: NFSM notified nationally April 1, 2007. Chhattisgarh state notification for implementation: Chhattisgarh participates in NFSM through agriculture ministry. State focus: Pulse production (26 districts), oilseed enhancement (12 districts). Components: Improved seed supply, input subsidy, extension training, demonstration plots. Implementation: District agriculture offices with farmer producer groups.
- CAG: CAG State Accounts Report 2024-25 audits NFSM implementation in Chhattisgarh. Audit findings: State budget contribution: ₹95 crores (FY 2024-25). Beneficiaries supported: 2.05 lakh farmers (pulses + oilseeds). Production outcomes: 18.5 lakh MT pulses, 4.2 lakh MT oilseeds verified. Seed distribution: 4,200 MT verified distribution. Fund utilization: 94% of allocation utilized. Productivity improvement: Verified through crop cutting experiments.
- PORTAL: National Food Security Mission featured on NFSM portal (nfsm.gov.in) and National Portal. Portal description: 'National scheme promoting food grain production to enhance national food security.' Portal features: State-wise performance (Chhattisgarh pulse/oilseed data), beneficiary statistics (2.05 lakh farmers), production metrics (18.5 lakh MT pulses, 4.2 lakh MT oilseeds), improved seed catalog. Chhattisgarh state portal (agriportal.cg.nic.in/nfsm) provides local implementation details. Cross-referenced with Food Security Act implementation.
