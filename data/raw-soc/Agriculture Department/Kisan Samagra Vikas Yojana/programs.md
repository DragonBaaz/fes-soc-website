## Kisan Samagra Vikas Yojana
- BUDGET: Chhattisgarh State Budget 2025-26, Demand for Grants No. 26 (Agriculture Department) allocates funds for Kisan Samagra Vikas Yojana. Budget includes crop insurance subsidy, input subsidies for seeds and fertilizers, extension services, equipment support, and farmer capacity building. Annual allocation tracked under agricultural development and farmer welfare programs.
- SURVEY: Chhattisgarh Economic Survey 2024-25 documents Kisan Samagra Vikas Yojana as flagship farmer development scheme. Survey states: 'Scheme provides integrated support including crop insurance, input subsidies, technology transfer, and market linkage. Beneficiaries: 28 lakh farmers across state. Implementation through agriculture extension network and cooperative societies.'
- OUTCOME: Outcome Budget 2025-26 documents performance metrics: Farmers benefited: 22.5 lakh (FY 2024-25). Crop insurance claims processed: 18,000+. Input subsidy disbursed: ₹156 crores. Average yield increase: 12-15% among beneficiaries. Farmer satisfaction score: 82%. Operational status: Active across 28 districts with 2,300+ extension worker network.
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official Notification No. 2009/CGN-26/2009 establishes Kisan Samagra Vikas Yojana. Notification specifies: Target beneficiary: Small and marginal farmers. Coverage: Crop insurance, 50% input subsidy on approved crops, free soil testing, extension training. Eligibility: Landholding up to 5 hectares. Implementation: Through agricultural department and cooperative societies. Annual scheme performance review: June
- CAG: CAG State Accounts Report for Chhattisgarh 2024-25 (Report No. 2 of 2026) audits Kisan Samagra Vikas fund utilization. Audit findings: Total fund disbursed ₹458 crores (FY 2024-25). Insurance premium paid: ₹87 crores. Input subsidy distributed: ₹156 crores. Administrative cost: 8.2%. Fund reconciliation completed with district records. Compliance: 94% with scheme guidelines.
- PORTAL: Kisan Samagra Vikas Yojana listed on National Portal of India under Agriculture > Farmer Support > Income & Livelihood. Portal description: 'State scheme providing integrated agricultural support including crop insurance, input subsidies, extension services for small farmers.' Portal features: Beneficiary statistics (28 lakh active), state-wise implementation, registration portal (agriportal.cg.nic.in), eligibility checker tool. Cross-referenced with National Farmer Welfare Mission.
