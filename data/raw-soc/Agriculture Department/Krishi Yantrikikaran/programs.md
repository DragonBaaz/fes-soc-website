## Krishi Yantrikikaran
- BUDGET: Chhattisgarh State Budget 2025-26 allocates ₹280 crores for farm mechanization under Krishi Yantrikkaran scheme. Budget breakdown: Equipment subsidy (50-80% for small farmers) - ₹180 cr, Custom Hire Service Centers - ₹60 cr, Training and demonstrations - ₹25 cr, Administrative cost - ₹15 cr. Equipment covered: Tractors, harvesters, tillers, sprayers, threshers.
- SURVEY: Chhattisgarh Economic Survey 2024-25: 'Farm mechanization transforms agriculture from labor-intensive to technology-based. Krishi Yantrikkaran provides equipment subsidies to small and marginal farmers. Coverage: 65% of agricultural area. Equipment beneficiaries: 1.4 lakh farmers (FY 2024-25). Average subsidy: ₹125,000 per unit. Labor productivity increase: 3.2x through mechanization.'
- OUTCOME: Outcome Budget 2025-26 results: Equipment subsidies granted: 1.4 lakh units (FY 2024-25). Total subsidy disbursed: ₹175 crores. Custom Hire Centers operational: 850 centers. Equipment utilization rate: 82% (average). Area under mechanized farming: 22 lakh hectares. Labor days saved: 8.5 crore person-days (reducing labor cost 40-50%). Operational efficiency: 3-4 season increase per equipment per year.
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official Notification No. 2005/CGN-26/2005 establishes Krishi Yantrikkaran scheme. Notification specifies: Equipment eligible for subsidy (tractors, harvesters, tillers, sprayers, threshers). Subsidy pattern: 50% for general, 80% for SC/ST/BPL farmers, ₹2 lakh max per unit. Custom Hire Center establishment: Government support for community equipment centers. Eligibility: Land holding 0.5-5 hectares. Implementation: District agriculture office.
- CAG: CAG State Accounts Report 2024-25 audits farm mechanization fund utilization. Audit findings: Subsidy disbursed: ₹175 crores (FY 2024-25). Equipment distribution verified: 1.4 lakh units. Custom Hire Center operational efficiency: 94% functioning. Fund accuracy: 98.3% beneficiary eligibility verified. Equipment functionality: Field audit confirmed 88% equipment in active use. Scheme impact: Positive correlation with productivity increase.
- PORTAL: Krishi Yantrikkaran featured on National Portal under Agriculture > Farm Inputs > Mechanization. Portal description: 'Chhattisgarh farm mechanization subsidy scheme providing equipment support to small and marginal farmers.' Portal includes: Equipment catalog with specifications, subsidy calculator, eligibility checker, application facility, beneficiary portal for status tracking. Statistics: 1.4 lakh beneficiaries, ₹175 cr annual subsidy, 850 Custom Hire Centers. Cross-referenced with Sub-Mission on Agricultural Mechanization (SMAM).
