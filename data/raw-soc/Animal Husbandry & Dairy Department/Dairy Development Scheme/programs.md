## Dairy Development Scheme
- BUDGET: State budget allocation for dairy enterprise development. Annual budget includes: dairy unit subsidy for shed construction, dairy animal purchase support, feed supplement cost, milk collection center infrastructure, dairy processing facility support, veterinary health programs. Allocated under value-added animal product schemes.
- SURVEY: Economic Survey: 'Dairy Development Scheme promotes milk production through enhanced infrastructure and value addition. Scheme supports dairy cooperatives, milk chilling centers, and processing units for livelihood improvement and rural economy strengthening.'
- OUTCOME: Implementation outcomes: Dairy entrepreneurs supported through subsidy. Production infrastructure created. Milk production volume increased. Cooperative dairy structure strengthened. Milk price realization improved for farmers. Value-added products developed (ghee, curd, paneer). Income generation documented.
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official notification for dairy development scheme. Notification outlines: Dairy unit subsidy eligibility, animal selection criteria, infrastructure requirements, beneficiary qualification, processing facility establishment support, cooperative formation guidelines, and milk quality standards.
- CAG: CAG State Accounts 2024-25 audits dairy scheme fund utilization. Audit examines: Subsidy disbursement to beneficiaries, infrastructure cost verification, animal health support expense, milk collection system establishment and operation, processing facility investment tracking, and scheme viability assessment.
- PORTAL: Dairy Development Scheme featured on National Portal under Agriculture > Animal Husbandry > Value Added Products. Portal provides subsidy details, eligibility criteria, application process, beneficiary statistics, and production metrics. Cross-referenced with national dairy development mission.
