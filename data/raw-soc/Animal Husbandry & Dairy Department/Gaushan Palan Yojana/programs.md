## Gaushan Palan Yojana
- BUDGET: State budget allocation for cow rearing support program. Annual budget includes: cattle shelter construction subsidy, feed provision support, breeding stock supply, veterinary care, heritage breed conservation, animal health insurance. Allocated under cattle development and livelihood security schemes.
- SURVEY: Economic Survey: 'Gaushan Palan Yojana promotes sustainable cattle rearing and indigenous breed conservation. Program supports farmers in cattle management while preserving heritage cattle breeds, contributing to agricultural sustainability and cultural preservation.'
- OUTCOME: Implementation outcomes: Beneficiaries enrolled in cattle rearing support. Cattle shelters constructed. Heritage breed population preserved. Feed and healthcare support provided. Breeding practices improved. Income generated through milk and animal sales. Cultural heritage breeds maintained.
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official notification establishing cattle rearing scheme. Notification specifies: Beneficiary eligibility criteria, subsidy amount for shelter, cattle breed selection (indigenous/heritage), feed assistance details, veterinary care provision, and performance monitoring framework for cattle management.
- CAG: CAG State Accounts 2024-25 audits cattle rearing scheme expenditure. Audit verifies: Shelter construction subsidy disbursement, cattle procurement cost, feed supply expense, veterinary service cost, breed conservation program effectiveness, and beneficiary scheme compliance.
- PORTAL: Gaushan Palan Yojana listed on National Portal under Agriculture > Animal Husbandry > Cattle Development. Portal provides scheme benefits, eligibility, subsidy details, breed information, and heritage conservation efforts. Cross-referenced with national indigenous breed preservation initiatives.
