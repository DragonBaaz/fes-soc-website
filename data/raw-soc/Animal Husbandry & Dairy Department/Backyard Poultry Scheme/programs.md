## Backyard Poultry Scheme
- BUDGET: Budget allocation for low-cost poultry rearing support. Annual budget includes: chick distribution subsidy, small-scale shelter construction assistance, feed supplement provision, veterinary extension service, disease prevention programs. Allocated under livelihood and nutritional security schemes.
- SURVEY: Economic Survey: 'Backyard Poultry Scheme provides rural households low-cost poultry rearing for nutritional security and supplementary income. Scheme targets marginal farmers and landless laborers with minimal investment models for household food production.'
- OUTCOME: Implementation outcomes: Beneficiaries enrolled in scheme. Chicks distributed and birds reared successfully. Household egg production increased. Nutritional intake improved through home production. Income generated through egg/bird sales. Disease incidence managed through extension support.
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official notification for backyard poultry scheme. Notification specifies: Target beneficiaries (marginal farmers, landless labor), chick subsidy amount, shelter construction support, feed assistance, veterinary service access, training and extension provision, and performance monitoring.
- CAG: CAG State Accounts 2024-25 audits backyard poultry support spending. Audit covers: Chick subsidy disbursement to beneficiaries, shelter construction cost verification, feed supply expense, veterinary service provision cost, farmer training program expenses, and scheme impact assessment.
- PORTAL: Backyard Poultry Scheme listed on National Portal under Agriculture > Animal Husbandry > Rural Livelihood. Portal provides scheme details, beneficiary eligibility, subsidy amounts, technical resources, and success stories. Referenced with national poultry development and nutritional security programs.
