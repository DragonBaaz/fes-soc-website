## Govt. Livestock / Poultry Farm
- BUDGET: State budget allocation for government livestock and poultry farm infrastructure. Annual budget includes: farm construction and maintenance, animal feed procurement, veterinary care, equipment purchase, staff salary, and genetic improvement programs. Allocated under animal husbandry development.
- SURVEY: Economic Survey: 'Government livestock and poultry farms serve as breeding centers and technology demonstration units. Infrastructure supports genetic improvement, quality animal stock multiplication, and farmer training for improved livestock management practices.'
- OUTCOME: Implementation outcomes: Government farms operational across districts. Quality animal stock produced and supplied to farmers. Genetic improvement demonstrated through superior progeny. Farmer training programs active. Production metrics tracked through farm records and animal performance data.
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official notification establishing government livestock and poultry farm operations. Notification specifies: Farm location and capacity, breeding objectives, animal types maintained, farmer access and eligibility criteria, and technical support provision framework.
- CAG: CAG State Accounts Report for Chhattisgarh 2024-25 audits government farm operations. Audit covers: Farm infrastructure maintenance expense, animal care cost, feed procurement, staff salary disbursement, genetic program effectiveness, and operational efficiency metrics.
- PORTAL: Government livestock farm infrastructure listed on National Portal under Agriculture > Animal Husbandry > Breeding Centers. Portal documents farm capacity, animal types, breeding programs, and farmer access mechanism. Cross-referenced with NITI Aayog livestock development initiatives.
