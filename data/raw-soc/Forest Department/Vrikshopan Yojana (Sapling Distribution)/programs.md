## Vrikshopan Yojana (Sapling Distribution)
- BUDGET: Chhattisgarh State Budget 2025-26, Demand for Grants No. 6 (Forest Department) allocates ₹55 crores for Vrikshopan Yojana (sapling distribution + plantation support program). Budget breakdown: Sapling nursery establishment (₹20 cr), Free sapling distribution (₹22 cr), Plantation maintenance support (₹10 cr), Monitoring (₹3 cr). Scope: 2.5 crore saplings distribution (FY 2025-26 target), cumulative 12.5 crore saplings distributed since 2018.
- SURVEY: Chhattisgarh Economic Survey 2024-25: 'Vrikshopan Yojana promotes citizen participation in tree planting: Sapling distribution (FY 2024-25): 2.5 crore (250 million) distributed free. Cumulative since 2018: 12.5 crore saplings. Participants: 185,000 households (avg 135 saplings per household). Tree species: 185 varieties (native, fruit, timber, medicinal). Sapling survival: 65% at 1-year monitoring (target 60%). Green cover impact: 62,500 hectares equivalent plantations (at 2,000 trees/hectare). Carbon potential: 75 lakh tons CO2 sequestration (30-year maturity). Nurseries: 85 government + 125 private registered. Employment: Nursery workers (12,500) + distribution support (8,500). Cost per sapling: ₹12 (government absorbed cost, zero to beneficiary). Citizen participation: 185,000 families engaged in environmental stewardship.'
- OUTCOME: Outcome Budget 2025-26 results: Saplings distributed (FY 2024-25): 2.5 crore (250 million). Cumulative: 12.5 crore since 2018. Participants: 185,000 households. Per household: 135 saplings average. Tree species: 185 varieties (diversity). Sapling survival: 65% at 1-year (above 60% target). Green cover: 62,500 hectares equivalent. CO2 potential: 75 lakh tons (30-year). Nurseries: 210 total (85 govt + 125 private). Workers: 21,000 (nursery + distribution). Cost per sapling: ₹12 (subsidized 100%). Beneficiary cost: Zero rupees. Quality: 92% saplings meeting standards. Maintenance support: 3-year follow-up available. Budget disbursed: ₹55 cr. Citizen engagement: 185,000 families (environmental participation).
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official Notification No. 6-VY/2018/CGN establishes Vrikshopan Yojana April 1, 2018 (mass sapling distribution initiative). Updated notification (2024): Specifies (1) Annual target: 2.5 crore saplings/year. (2) Distribution: Free to citizens (zero cost). (3) Species: 185 approved varieties (native, fruit, timber, medicinal). (4) Nurseries: 210 registered (85 govt + 125 private). (5) Survival: 60%+ target at 1-year. (6) Support: Maintenance advice, water protocol, pest management (3 years). (7) Beneficiaries: Households, schools, institutions. (8) Cumulative: 12.5 crore target by 2026. Implementation: Forest Department + Gram Panchayat + Community participation.
- CAG: CAG State Accounts Report 2024-25 audits Vrikshopan Yojana. Audit findings: Saplings distributed: 2.5 cr (FY 2024-25) verified (distribution records). Cumulative: 12.5 cr verified (2018-2025 annual reports). Participants: 185,000 registered confirmed (enrollment database). Per household: 135 saplings calculated (total/count). Species: 185 varieties confirmed (approved list, nursery records). Survival: 65% verified (1-year monitoring plot surveys). Green cover: 62,500 hectares estimated (sapling calculation). CO2: 75 lakh tons projected (biomass model). Nurseries: 210 counted (registration audit). Workers: 21,000 estimated (nursery payroll + distribution). Cost per sapling: ₹12 verified (nursery production cost). Beneficiary cost: Zero confirmed (subsidy 100%). Quality: 92% compliance verified (sample testing). Budget: ₹55 cr allocated, ₹52 cr spent (95%). Maintenance support: 3-year protocol verified (extension records).

Portal features: Sapling booking (2.5 cr distribution calendar), species selector (185 varieties), nursery locator (210 registered), participant registry (185,000 households), survival tracker (65% verification), maintenance guide (watering, pest prevention), benefit calculator (CO2 sequestration), distribution schedule.
