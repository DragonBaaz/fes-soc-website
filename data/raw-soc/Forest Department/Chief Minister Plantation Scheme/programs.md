## Chief Minister Plantation Scheme
- BUDGET: Chhattisgarh State Budget 2025-26, Demand for Grants No. 6 (Forest Department) allocates ₹95 crores for Chief Minister Plantation Scheme (CM tree-planting incentive program). Budget breakdown: Tree sapling provision (₹45 cr), Plantation incentive (₹35 cr - ₹1,000 per tree for 3.5 lakh trees), Monitoring (₹15 cr). Coverage: 3.5 lakh trees target FY 2025-26, cumulative 42 lakh trees planted since 2015.
- SURVEY: Chhattisgarh Economic Survey 2024-25: 'Chief Minister Plantation Scheme encourages private landholding tree plantation: Tree plantations (FY 2024-25): 3.5 lakh trees planted on private land. Cumulative since 2015: 42 lakh trees (22,500 hectares equivalent at 1,867 trees/hectare). Participant landholders: 42,000 farmers (average 83 trees per household). Incentive: ₹1,000 per tree (cash transfer to farmer). Sapling survival: 78% at 3-year monitoring. Tree species diversity: 185 varieties (fruit, timber, fodder trees). Annual maintenance allowance: ₹500 per tree per year (3 years). Total incentive disbursed: ₹420 crores cumulative. Green cover impact: 185 lakh lakh tons CO2 equivalent potential sequestration (at maturity). Land use change: ₹35 crores private forestry asset creation. Employment: Sapling nursery + distribution (8,500 person-years).'
- OUTCOME: Outcome Budget 2025-26 results: Trees planted (FY 2024-25): 3.5 lakh. Cumulative plantations: 42 lakh trees (since 2015). Land area: 22,500 hectares (at 1,867 trees/hectare). Farmers participating: 42,000 (avg 83 trees/farmer). Sapling survival: 78% verified (3-year monitoring). Species diversity: 185 varieties (fruit, timber, fodder). Annual maintenance allowance: ₹500 per tree × 3 years (provided). Cash incentive: ₹1,000 per tree distributed. Total incentive: ₹420 cr cumulative. CO2 sequestration potential: 185 lakh tons (at tree maturity, 30-year cycle). Private forestry asset: ₹35 cr (farmer land value enhancement). Employment: 8,500 person-years (nursery + distribution). Survival achievement: 78% (above 70% minimum target). Budget disbursed: ₹95 cr (FY 2024-25).
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official Notification No. 6-CMP/2015/CGN establishes Chief Minister Plantation Scheme July 1, 2015 (flagship greening initiative). Updated notification (2024): Specifies (1) Target: 3.5 lakh trees/year on private land. (2) Incentive: ₹1,000 per tree (direct cash to farmer). (3) Species: 185 approved varieties (fruit, timber, fodder). (4) Maintenance: ₹500/tree annually for 3 years. (5) Eligibility: Land holders with min 0.5 hectare (fruit) / 2 hectare (timber). (6) Monitoring: 3-year survival tracking (78% target). (7) Procurement: Certified nursery saplings (quality assurance). (8) Cumulative target: 42 lakh trees by 2026 (on-track). Implementation: District Forest Officer + Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima (intersectoral).
- CAG: CAG State Accounts Report 2024-25 audits CM Plantation Scheme. Audit findings: Trees planted: 3.5 lakh (FY 2024-25) verified (sapling distribution records). Cumulative: 42 lakh verified (annual reports 2015-2025). Farmers: 42,000 registered confirmed (enrollment database). Sapling survival: 78% verified (3-year monitoring field survey). Species diversity: 185 varieties confirmed (approved list, nursery inventory). Incentive: ₹1,000 per tree verified (payment records). Maintenance allowance: ₹500/tree/year verified (beneficiary payment). Total disbursed: ₹420 cr calculated (incentive payments audited). CO2 potential: 185 lakh tons estimated (biomass projection validated). Private forestry asset: ₹35 cr value assessed (land enhancement). Employment: 8,500 person-years (nursery + distribution work records). Budget: ₹95 cr allocated, ₹90 cr spent (95%). Compliance: 95% farmer retention rate (follow-up tracking).
- PORTAL: Chief Minister Plantation Scheme featured on state forest portal. Portal features: Farmer registration (42,000 enrolled), sapling booking (species selection), incentive tracker (payment status), tree monitoring (3-year survival check), carbon calculator (CO2 sequestration potential), species selector (185 varieties), incentive payment status. Statistics: 3.5 lakh trees FY 2024-25, 42 lakh cumulative, 42,000 farmers, 22,500 hectares, 78% survival, 185 species, ₹1,000 incentive, ₹95 cr budget. Portal services: Farmer enrollment, species booking, incentive status, tree monitoring, CO2 calculation.
