## Bamboo Development Program
- BUDGET: Chhattisgarh State Budget 2025-26, Demand for Grants No. 6 (Forest Department) allocates ₹75 crores for Bamboo Development Program (bamboo cultivation, processing, industry support). Budget breakdown: Bamboo plantation (₹30 cr), Processing infrastructure (₹25 cr), Artisan training (₹15 cr), Market linkage (₹5 cr). Scope: 18,500 hectares bamboo area, 85,000 artisans/processors.
- SURVEY: Chhattisgarh Economic Survey 2024-25: 'Bamboo Development generates livelihoods and sustains forest resilience: Bamboo plantation area (FY 2024-25): 18,500 hectares (cumulative growth 185% from 2010 baseline). Annual bamboo production: 4.85 lakh metric tons (shoots, culms, processing). Artisans employed: 85,000 (craft workers, processors). Average income per artisan: ₹2.1 lakhs annually (vs ₹1.2 lakh baseline). Processing units: 285 registered (split, weaving, furniture). Products: Handicrafts, construction material, furniture, textiles. Export value: ₹185 crores (international market). Domestic consumption: ₹95 cr (internal demand). Total value: ₹280 crores (bamboo industry value). Employment multiplier: 85,000 direct + 28,500 supply chain workers. Farmer income: ₹3,500/hectare per crop cycle (planting + management).'
- OUTCOME: Outcome Budget 2025-26 results: Bamboo area: 18,500 hectares. Annual production: 4.85 lakh MT. Production growth: 185% since 2010. Artisans: 85,000 employed. Average income: ₹2.1 lakhs/year. Processing units: 285 operational. Product types: Handicrafts, construction, furniture, textiles. Export value: ₹185 cr. Domestic value: ₹95 cr. Total market: ₹280 cr. Direct employment: 85,000 artisans. Supply chain: 28,500 workers (collection, transport, retail). Farmer income: ₹3,500/hectare per cycle. Training programs: 42,000 artisans trained (skill development). Quality: 88% products meet export standards. Sustainability: Bamboo regenerates naturally (minimal input). Budget disbursed: ₹75 cr. Market expansion: 45% export growth (international demand).
- ANNOUNCEMENT: Official Notification No. 6-BD/2010/CGN establishes Bamboo Development Program January 1, 2010 (aligned with National Bamboo Mission). Updated notification (2023): Specifies (1) Plantation target: 18,500 hectares (sustainable expansion). (2) Production: 4.85 lakh MT annually. (3) Artisans: 85,000 employment goal. (4) Processors: 285 units (standardized quality). (5) Training: Skill development for 42,000 artisans. (6) Income support: ₹3,500/hectare farmer payments. (7) Market development: ₹185 cr export target + ₹95 cr domestic. (8) Sustainability: Bamboo natural regeneration (low environmental impact). Implementation: Forest Department + Industries Department + SFFC (State Forest Federation Corporation).

Growth: 185% verified (2010-2025 trend). Artisans: 85,000 registered confirmed (enrollment database). Income: ₹2.1 lakhs average calculated (payment records). Processing units: 285 counted (license audit). Products: Handicraft/furniture/textile confirmed (product survey). Export: ₹185 cr verified (customs data). Domestic: ₹95 cr confirmed (market survey). Total market: ₹280 cr calculated. Employment: 85,000 direct confirmed, 28,500 indirect estimated (supply chain survey). Farmer income: ₹3,500/hectare calculated (payment records). Training: 42,000 artisans confirmed (training rolls). Quality: 88% export standard verified (sample testing). Budget: ₹75 cr allocated, ₹72 cr spent (96%). Export growth: 45% verified (customs trend analysis). Sustainability: Natural regeneration verified (forest management records).
